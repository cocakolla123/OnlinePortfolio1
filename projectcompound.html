Project Compound

Principles Of Engineering

August 29, 2019 - September 11, 2019

For the Compound Machine Project, we were tasked to make a compound machine that would perform a simple task by using only one human input (ie turning a wheel). Some constraints we had were that we could only use vex or fishertex parts, we had to incorporate at least four simple machines into our design, and we had a two week deadline. Our Team decided to make a scale model of a conveyor belt that can transport your groceries around the house. the four simple machines we used are a wheel driving axle, a compound gear train, a belt and pulley system, and a belt and sprocket system. Our one input is turning a crank that would then move the gear train, which would turn the belt and pulley system that starts the conveyor belt.
