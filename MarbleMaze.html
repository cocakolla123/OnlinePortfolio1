Introduction To Engineering Design

April 2019

The Marble Maze project was an individual project in which everyone had to design a game that used a marble and could be played inside a cardboard board box. The only materials we were allowed to use were: a popsicle stick, a skewer, a peice of cardboard, and a cardboard box. Once we finish designing the game, it would be 3d printed by students in the CIM (Computer Integrated Manufacturing) class. My idea was to create a marble drop game that would fall into 3 different point categories. The hardest hole would be 500 points and the easier holes would be worth 100 points. I created obstacles using the skewer and the Popsicle stick that would make it harder to go into the middle hole. I created side barriers so the marble would not fall out of the box if you tilt it too much. You have to drop the marble through the yellow tunnel on the top and your main goal is to get it into the small hole in the middle.
