E.S. Palm

Introduction To Engineering Design

April 2019

For the Palm Pilot Project, everyone had to reverse engineer the original Palm Pilot and model it in inventor. After that, everyone formed teams of up to 4 and came up with an idea to make the Palm Pilot used in a specific field. All the teams had to have different ideas because every innovation would be a part of a product family. Our Palm Pilot was an essentials and security palm pilot named "E.S. Palm" Some features that our Palm Pilot consisted of was a Nikon DSLR camera for taking high quality photos which also starts automatically recording when an item of yours is stolen or the palm pilot itself is stolen, a stylus that is also a microphone for voice recognition and voice recording, a bluetooth module to connect to very tiny tracking devices that can stick on to anything and to connect to personal gadgets, and a cardholder, made of steel that can only be opened through voice recognition.

Individual Parts

Stylus comes with audio sensor to capture voice and you can turn it on by command. It also automatically saves all of the recordings and savs it to the cloud for easy recovery.

Nikon DSLR 4K Camera Lens for taking high quality pictures and videos. Activates automatic recording when palm pilot is stolen from owner.

The provided bluetooth module is used to connect with personal items like wallets, phones, etc. It can track your belongings and pinpoint their exact locations.

Upgraded 1080p screen to ensure quality graphics and colorful user interface.

Cardholder that is sealed and can only be opened using voice recognition and by answering personal questions.

Comes with three bluetooth tracking devices that can stick on to personal belongings that are not electronic. EX: watches, jewelry, etc. (You can buy more than three
