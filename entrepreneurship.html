Principles of Engineering

October 14, 2019 - October 24, 2019

In the mechanical winch project, we made a winch to lift objects and we created a model for it in netlogo to simulate its performance. I mainly worked on the netlogo program, but I helped on brainstorming the design for our winch. In our netlogo simulation, we were to simulate a mechanical winch lifting up a weight. This simulation would have factors such as drum size, input power, and mass of the object. We had labels that would display the angular speed and linear speed of the winch. Our actual design was not very effecient with an effeciency of just around 5.06%. This is most probably due to the fact that we are using vex parts, so our gears are not as smooth as they ca be and the overall stability of the machine is not as good.
